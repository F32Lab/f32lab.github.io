What DataThread
can download for you

If the data is on the web and there's no anti-bot system or CAPTCHA blocking it, we can probably automate it.

e-commerce

Product prices

Monitor prices of specific products on one or more sites. Receive an updated Excel file every morning with current price and change.

// output:
product, price, delta
Miele Robot, CHF 349.00, -12%
Dyson V15, CHF 699.00, +0%
portal

Invoices & documents

Logs into your supplier portal with your credentials and automatically downloads monthly PDF invoices, renamed and organised.

// output:
invoice_2026_01.pdf
invoice_2026_02.pdf
invoice_2026_03.pdf
real-estate

Property listings

Collects all new listings matching your criteria (area, price, sqm) from one or more portals and sends them in CSV.

// output:
address, price, sqm, link
Lugano Centre, 450k, 85, ...

stock

Product availability

Checks if a product is back in stock on a site and notifies you with updated data.

// output:
product, available, url

⚠ One important thing

DataThread works on sites that don't actively block bots. If a site uses CAPTCHA, Cloudflare Bot Fight or aggressive anti-scraping systems, we may not be able to collect it — we'll tell you upfront, at no cost.

How it works

Simple from start
to finish.

No code to write on your end.

01

Describe the site and data

URL, what you want to extract and how often. If login required, you share credentials privately afterwards.

02

We check for anti-bot protections and tell you what's possible — free and no commitment.

03

We build the scraper

Once the quote is confirmed, we set everything up. Usually live within a few days.

04

Data arriving

You receive files at the time you chose. Maintenance included if the site changes structure.
